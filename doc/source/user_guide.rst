Introduction
------------

The "oc_lettings_site" is a web application designed to help users browse available lettings, view user profiles, and manage their accounts. Here's a quick overview of the main features:

- **Browsing Lettings:** Explore available lettings and view detailed information.
- **Viewing Profiles:** Learn more about other users by viewing their profiles.
- **Manage Profiles and lettings:** Create or update a profile or a letting to personalize your experience.
- **Contacting a Letting:** Use the provided contact information to inquire about a letting.
- **Managing Your Account:** Update your profile details and preferences.

Browsing Lettings
------------------

1. **Home Page:**
   - Visit the home page to see a list of all available lettings.
   - Click on a letting to view more details.

2. **Letting Details:**
   - On the letting details page, find information such as the title, address, and contact details.
   - Use the provided contact information to inquire about the letting.

Viewing Profiles
----------------

1. **Profiles Page:**
   - Navigate to the profiles section to see a list of user profiles.
   - Click on a profile to view more details.

2. **Profile Details:**
   - On the profile details page, learn more about the user, including their favorite city.

Manage Profiles and lettings
-------------------

1. **Login:**
   - Access admin and login.

1. **Manage:**
   - Follow the prompts to manage Profiles and lettings.

Contacting a Letting
----------------------

1. **Contact Information:**
   - On the letting details page, find the contact information of the letting owner or agent.
   - Use the provided email or phone number to inquire about the letting.

Managing Your Account
----------------------

1. **Profile Settings:**
   - Visit the "admin" page to update your details, change your password, or set your favorite city.

2. **Logout:**
   - Click on "Logout" to securely log out of your account.

That's it! You are now ready to explore and make the most of the features offered by the "oc_lettings_site" project. If you have any questions or encounter issues, refer to the :ref:`contact` section for assistance.
